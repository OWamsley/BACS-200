Yoyo Communities

If you get into yoyoing, it can be hard to find others that share your interest. Sometimes, you can find local groups to yoyo with, so it is worth looking online for that. For most, they will be hard to find, so here are some online communities where you can talk about yoyoing and ask questions.

reddit.com/r/throwers

This subreddit on reddit is a great smaller community full of people that are willing to help and share anything yoyo. Here you can find buy/sell pages, where you can sell or find used yoyos, or just look at everybody's latest and greatest tricks.
Click Here

YoyoExpert Forums

This forum connected to a popular online yoyo store has been around for a long time, and is active. This is also a great resource for finding helpful people, or showing off your newest tricks.


Click Here

Youtube

While not as much of a cohesive community, there are too many amazing yoyo videos to not point yoyoers to youtube. You can find many videos of compeition routines, trick tutorials, and other yoyo videos.
