Wealth Gap in America

The wage gap between the rich and the poor has been growing for a very long time now.

  • America’s top 10 percent on average make 9 time as much as the bottom 90%.
  • An even more stark gap lies between the top 1 percent and the bottom 90%. On average, they earn 39 times more than the bottom 90%
  • Lastly, the top .1% earn 196 times more than the bottom 90 percent.

The rich get richer.

The three richest men in America own more together than the bottom 50% of americans. Jeff Bezos’ total wealth is 114 billion dollars. Jeff Bezos spending 1.7 million dollars is like an average american spending just one dollar.

The wealth gap is so staggering, it can be hard to comprehend the difference in wealth.
