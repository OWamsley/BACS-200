What is the purpose of art? For many, it's about expressing yourself and sharing that with others. This, Bob Ross does masterfully.

Bob Ross is an inspiring individual for many reasons.

  • Friendliness - Art can be an intimidating hobby, but many would feel comfortable sharing art at any level with Bob.
  • Talented - Bob Ross leads by example. All of his art is beautiful to look at.
  • Impact - There is an amazingly large amount of people that know of Bob Ross, and have enjoyed his show and art.
  • Smile - Bob Ross has a nice smile, and he shows how important and effective being friendly to others is.
  • Squirell - For a while, he nursed a baby squirrel on his tv show.

There are many figures on tv that try to showcase how important being nice to others. Bob Ross' wholesome show is among the best. If it weren't for role models such as Bob Ross or Mr. Rogers, modern society wouldn't exist how it does today.
